Photography Tips

  • Always confirm your f/stop.
  • Format your card before you start shooting
  • Ensure your lighting is adequate

Photography schedule of a shoot day (for potraits)

TIMINGS ACTIVITY DETAILS
Morning Arrive Early Get to the location with time to set up and prepare.
Set up gear Arrange and test all equipment (lighting, camera settings, props).
Brief Clients/Models Go over the shoot plan, mood, and expectations.
Midday Begin Shooting Start with warm-up shots to test lighting and composition.
Capture Variety Keep track of time (e.g., golden hour if shooting outdoors).
Afternoon Review Photos Take short breaks to review images and adjust as necessary.
Continue Shooting Capture remaining planned shots and finalize the session.
Pack Up Gear Securely pack all gear after the shoot is done.
